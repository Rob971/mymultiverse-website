What it does

Built for families who cook together

Ammò reduces the friction between planning meals, buying ingredients, and coordinating who does what.

  • Shared grocery list — add, check off, and sync items across household members
  • Weekly meal plan — organize breakfasts, lunches, and dinners for the week ahead
  • Household invites — owners invite family with roles (admin, editor, viewer)
  • Local AI assistant — suggestions for groceries and meals, clearly separated from your editable lists
  • Offline-first — keep working when connectivity is spotty; sync when you are back online
  • Multilingual — eight locales in the app, designed for diverse households
