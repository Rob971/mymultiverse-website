Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MyMultiverse (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you visit mymultiverse.app, contact us, or use our mobile applications, including Ammò (“the App”). Ammò is a product of MyMultiverse.

1. Data controller

MyMultiverse
Contact: roberto.cornano@mymultiverse.app
Website: https://mymultiverse.app

2. Information we collect

Website visitors

Our static company website does not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics cookies. Standard web server and hosting logs (such as IP address, browser type, and requested pages) may be processed by our hosting provider for security and operations.

Ammò app users

When you create an account and use Ammò, we may process:

  • Account information — email address and authentication identifiers managed through our auth provider
  • Profile information — display name or preferences you choose to provide
  • Household data — household membership, roles (owner, admin, editor, viewer), and invite metadata
  • App content — grocery list items, meal plan entries, and related notes you or your household save in the app
  • Device tokens — push notification tokens on Android (and iOS when enabled) so we can deliver household-related alerts you opt into
  • Diagnostic data — crash reports and limited technical logs to fix bugs and improve stability

We do not sell your personal information to advertisers or data brokers.

3. How we use information

We use the information above to:

  • Provide, sync, and secure Ammò features for you and your household
  • Send transactional emails (for example, household invitations) and push notifications you expect
  • Authenticate users and enforce household roles and access controls
  • Respond to support requests and legal obligations
  • Monitor reliability, diagnose crashes, and improve the App

4. Legal bases (EEA/UK users)

Where applicable, we rely on: performance of a contract (providing the App), legitimate interests (security, product improvement), and consent (where required, such as optional notifications).

5. Third-party services

We use reputable infrastructure providers. Data may be processed by:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, realtime sync, and serverless functions for Ammò backend services
  • Firebase (Google) — on Android: Crashlytics for crash reporting and Cloud Messaging for push notifications
  • Resend — transactional email delivery for household invitations
  • Firebase Hosting — serving this website and App Link fallback pages

These providers process data on our behalf under their terms and security measures. We configure access controls (including database row-level security) so household data is visible only to authorized members.

6. Data retention

We retain account and household data while your account is active and as needed to provide the service. When you delete your account through the App, we delete or anonymize associated personal data within a reasonable period, subject to backups and legal retention requirements.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, export, restrict, or delete your personal data, and to object to certain processing.

  • Export and delete — available in Ammò account settings
  • Contact us — email roberto.cornano@mymultiverse.app for privacy requests

You may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8. Security

We use HTTPS, authenticated APIs, role-based access, and industry-standard practices. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; please use a strong, unique password and keep your device updated.

9. Children

Ammò is intended for household use by adults who manage accounts and invites. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without appropriate parental consent. Contact us if you believe a child has provided us data.

10. International transfers

Our providers may process data in countries other than your own. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses.

11. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the revised version on this page with an updated “Last updated” date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance where permitted by law.

12. Contact

Questions about this policy: roberto.cornano@mymultiverse.app or our contact page.
