About MyMultiverse

A small studio with a long engineering track record

Why we exist

MyMultiverse was founded to build software that fits real households and teams — tools that are reliable, respectful of privacy, and pleasant to use every day. We are not a hype-driven agency; we are a focused studio that ships products and systems we stand behind.

Founder-led engineering

MyMultiverse is Roberto Cornano’s software studio. With 14 years of professional Java development, he delivers the web applications, mobile clients, and automation behind products like Ammò.

Ammò was co-created with Carola ZENO, who is the app’s main creator — she defined the product vision and household experience; Roberto is co-creator and lead engineer.

That experience shapes how MyMultiverse works: explicit architecture, tested code paths, and infrastructure choices that balance speed with maintainability.

Products, not projects only

Ammò is our first product — household nutrition logistics for shared grocery lists, meal planning, and family invites. It was created by Carola ZENO (main creator) and Roberto Cornano (co-creator). Future apps will follow the same bar for quality, accessibility, and trust.

MyMultiverse also takes on selective engineering work where there is a strong fit. Contact us for collaboration or product inquiries.

Values

  • Resilience — offline-capable apps and operable backends
  • Respect — user data stays user-controlled; we do not sell personal information to advertisers
  • Craft — design systems, localization, and attention to detail in every release
