Capabilities

End-to-end engineering

MyMultiverse combines product thinking with hands-on implementation across the layers that matter for durable software.

  • Web applications Modern UIs, REST and RPC APIs, authentication, and role-based access.
  • Microservices & backends JVM services, event-driven workflows, Postgres with row-level security.
  • Mobile (KMP) Kotlin Multiplatform with Compose Multiplatform for Android and iOS.
  • DevOps & CI/CD Gradle pipelines, Firebase hosting and distribution, automated verification.
  • Security & compliance Privacy policies, OAuth integrations, and accountable data handling.
  • UX & design systems Consistent components, dark theme, wide layouts, and accessible patterns.
